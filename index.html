SMARTCURRENCY

An AI-powered money recognition assistant for visually impaired users. SmartCurrency uses computer vision to identify Philippine peso banknotes in real time and announces them aloud.

HOW TO USE

  • Tap Start camera and allow camera access
  • Hold a peso bill flat in good lighting
  • Fit it inside the dashed box
  • Tap the gold Scan button
  • Use Auto scan for hands-free mode

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

  • Use good lighting — bright daylight or room light works best
  • Hold the bill flat, not folded or crumpled
  • Keep the camera ~20cm (8 inches) from the bill
  • Show the front of the bill when possible
  • If scan fails, tap Scan again — it will retry

VOICE COMMANDS

  • "check" or "scan" — identify the bill
  • "repeat" — say the last result again
  • "clear" — clear history
  • "stop" — stop the camera

SUPPORTED BILLS

₱20 (orange) · ₱50 (red) · ₱100 (purple) · ₱200 (green) · ₱500 (yellow) · ₱1000 (blue)
