SMARTCURRENCY

An AI-powered money recognition assistant for visually impaired users. SmartCurrency uses computer vision to identify Philippine peso banknotes in real time and announces them aloud.

HOW TO USE

  • Tap Start camera and allow camera access
  • Point at a peso bill, fitting it inside the dashed box
  • Tap Scan or say "check" — it announces the value
  • Use Auto scan for hands-free continuous scanning

VOICE COMMANDS

  • "check" or "scan" — identify the bill
  • "repeat" — say the last result again
  • "clear" — clear history
  • "stop" — stop the camera

SUPPORTED BILLS

₱20, ₱50, ₱100, ₱200, ₱500, ₱1000 Philippine peso banknotes

ACCESSIBILITY

Designed for visually impaired users. Features include voice commands, text-to-speech feedback, large buttons, high contrast colors, and haptic vibration feedback.
